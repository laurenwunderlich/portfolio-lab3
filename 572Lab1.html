Blackout: Gen-Z, Media Censorship, and Uprisings in Nepal

For this story map, I investigated the topic of social media censorship by discussing the September 2025 riots in Nepal over the ban of 26 social media apps. I was inspired to choose this topic after hearing about the event on the social media platform TikTok; realizing that this story had not made U.S paper headlines and thus social media was used as a tool by protesters on the ground to reach a worldwide news audience. In recognizing that my intended audience are interested in and familiar with social media, and those who may even find this story via social media, I designed the map to be a fast-paced, scrolling experience. This was done using ArcGIS Story Maps as well as ArcGIS Pro and Online. The datasets I used to support this story cartographically and visually came from the Pew Research Center, Jahangirnagar University, and the 2023 World Press Freedom Index from Reporters Without Borders.
