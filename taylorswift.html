Question... Does Taylor Have A Favorite City?

Created as my final project for Introduction to Cartography, this map explores the locations that Taylor Swift has stopped (as of May 2023) on tour dates and the frequency of stops. I was inspired to make the map in anticipation of attending the Eras Tour, which I did in June of 2023 after somehow scoring face-value tickets. Created using ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator, the map includes tour data as well as fun facts that play off of song titles from throughout her discography.
