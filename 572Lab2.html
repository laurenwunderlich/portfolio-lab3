That 70's Map

For this map, I was tasked with the design of a tileset that reflected the aesthetic of the popular That 70's Show and the visual style of the era. The key was to keep in mind form, color, type, and texture. I designed this map to include many iconic elements of the 1970's such as flowers, records, and denim that appear at differing zoom levels. With inspiration photos ranging from fashion magazine to image stills from the episodes, I realized quickly that bold color was going to be my greatest design tool for building mood. I utilized MapBox Studio and Adobe Illustrator to create this tileset.
